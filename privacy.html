Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026 · Effective immediately

This policy explains how Unc's Journal collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We keep it simple — we collect only what we need, we don't sell your data, and you can delete your account at any time.

1. Who We Are

Unc's Journal ("we", "us", "our") is a trading journal platform accessible at uncsjournal.com. We provide tools for traders to log trades, track performance, and receive AI-powered coaching.

2. Information We Collect

Account information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and password (encrypted).

Profile information: Information you voluntarily provide such as account size, risk percentage, trading style, instruments traded, broker, and trading goals.

Trade data: All trade entries you log including symbols, dates, directions, results, notes, and screenshots URLs.

Usage data: Basic information about how you use the app (e.g. features accessed) to help us improve the product.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the trading journal service
  • To generate AI-powered trade analysis and coaching (data is sent to Anthropic's Claude API)
  • To calculate performance statistics on your dashboard
  • To send you account-related emails (confirmation, password reset)
  • To improve the platform based on usage patterns

We do not use your trading data for advertising, nor do we sell it to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the platform:

  • Supabase — database and authentication. Your data is stored on Supabase servers in Frankfurt, Germany (EU). Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — trade analysis and coaching. Trade details are sent to Claude to generate feedback. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare — website hosting and security. Cloudflare Privacy Policy

5. Data Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase's infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We implement row-level security so you can only access your own data.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your trade data at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@uncsjournal.com.

8. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for authentication (keeping you logged in). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

Unc's Journal is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or via a notice in the app. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
